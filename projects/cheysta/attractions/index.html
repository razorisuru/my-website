Places to See

Silver Temple

A place to discover the traditions and the rituals of Theravadha Buddhism. The silver temple has the history starting from 2nd century BC while the ancient paintings and the sculpture has some news about the Gautama Buddha’s life.

Yapahuwa

Witness the architectural beauty of the Citadel of the medieval Sri Lanka’s capital Yapahuwa, which has been skillfully built high in a 100m high granite rock has to be lead from the ornamental stair case. The skillful ancient painting and the sculptures are something that will leave the viewers amazed.

Panduwasnuwara

Observe the ruins of the controlling center of the great king, king Parakramabahu of 12th century. Among the ruins scattered over 20 hectares you can see ruins of the palace, monasteries, image houses, dagobas and monks' living quarters, carved pillars, guard stones, and other ancient constructions.
