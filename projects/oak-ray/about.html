About Us

Since 1999

A new level of luxury hospitality.

  • Every great city deserves a truly great international hotel - a special place that reflects the importance and the beauty of its location with world-class luxury and the very finest service.
  • We are delighted to welcome you to the enchanting capital of Sri Lanka - a precious jewel in the Indian Ocean, with a glittering cultural heritage and a long, proud history.
  • The hotel benefits from a prime location by the sea that is in the heart of the business district and buzzing social hotspot.

Colombo, the commercial capital of Sri Lanka, has a long history as an important port on the ancient east-west trade routes, and has been variously ruled by the Portuguese, Dutch and British. That heritage is reflected in its often charming architecture and unique culture.
